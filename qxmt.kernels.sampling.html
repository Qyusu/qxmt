Parameters:

  • n_qubits (int) – number of qubits

  • state_pattern (str, optional) – pattern of the observable state. Defaults to “01”.

list of all possible observable states

list[str]

Parameters:

  • n_qubits (int) – number of qubits

  • shots (int) – number of shots

  • state_pattern (str, optional) – pattern of the observable state. Defaults to “01”.
